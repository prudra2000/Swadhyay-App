Nagar gavaiya pase

નાગર ગવૈયા પાસે
P.6 • Vat 162 
(162) nāgara gavaiyā pāse 1 pāvaḻiyāmāṃ= pāḻiyāmāṃ, khāṃbhīmāṃ. $2 eḻe= phogaṭa, vyartha, nirarthaka, vṛthā.
